About me

    I am a third-year PhD student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at Ohio State University. I am pursuing my PhD under the supervision of Dr. Xiaorui Wang. My main research Interests is in Embedded and Real-time Systems. I also explore topics like Data Center Computing, Computer Architecture and Systems and cyber-physical systems.
