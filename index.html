About Me

I am a third-year PhD student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at Ohio State University. I am pursuing my PhD under the supervision of Dr. Xiaorui Wang. My main research interests are in Embedded and Real-time Systems, Data Center Computing, Computer Architecture, and Cyber-Physical Systems.
