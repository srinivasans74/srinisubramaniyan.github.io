About me

    I am a PhD student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at the Ohio State University. I am currently working as a Research Assistant at the Power-Aware Computer Systems (PACS) Laboratory. My area of interest spans Digital Design, Computer Architecture, GPU Scheduling and Power optimization. Prior to this I was working as a Junior Research fellow at the Centre for Heterogeneous and Intelligent Processing Systems in Bangalore, India.
